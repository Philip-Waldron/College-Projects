Rockvaria is a new festival planned to be held for three days in Munich, Germany for 2015!

Following in the tradition of Wacken Open Air and Rock am Ring, Rockvaria is another festival that will bring together a lineup of well-known international rock bands bands with rock music fans from across Germany, neighboring European countries, and many other locations! It's planned to be held at the OlympiaPark from May 29th to May 31st.

The 2015 festival will be headlined by a series of well-known American rock and metal bands including Incubus, Faith No More, and Five Finger Death Punch. Additionally, Metallica and KISS, two beloved classic metal bands with huge international followings, will be performing over the three day period! The headlining list also features several well-known European bands including England's hugely popular Muse!

Tickets are on sale now via Ticketmaster at an 'early bird' price of 169 Euros, which includes admission for all three days of the festival!

The iTunes Festival is a full-volume celebration of live music with performances from some of the biggest names in music! If you were lucky enough, you could've won tickets to see these performances live in person, otherwise the event was broadcast live on Apple TV.


The festival returned to the Roundhouse this September, presenting 30 nights of incredible live shows from names such as deadmau5, The Script and many more!

Not only was the festival held in London, but for the first time a five day festival premiered at the Moody Theater, Austin, Texas from March 11th to March 15th, boasting big names such as Coldplay and Imagine Dragons!
